# Fake Level Name

Have you ever come across an online level name that you absolutely disliked, found boring, or just wanted to change for a video or a meme? Don't worry, this mod allows you to locally rename any online level to whatever you prefer, giving you complete control over your saved list.

Mod idea by waffle.
